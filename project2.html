Angela's Recipe for the Best

Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • ¾ cup warm milk
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • 2 ¼ teaspoons yeast
  • ¼ cup granulated sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • ¼ cup butter
  • 3 cups bread flour

For the filling:

  1. 2/3 cup dark brown sugar
  2. 1 ½ tablespoons ground cinnamon
  3. ¼ cup butter
